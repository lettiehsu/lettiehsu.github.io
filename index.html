如诗淡泊自芬芳

所谓美人，以花为貌。以鸟为声，以月为神，以柳为态，以玉为骨，以冰雪为肌，以秋水为姿，以诗词为心。 女人的美丽如自然诗画，更如同一种心境，怡静，淡泊！精灵般美丽的女人仿佛是林间清晨，微雨初歇，婷妍交辉； 仿佛是水晶中的绿幽灵，虽没有惊世的炫彩，却灵气逼人；这份妩媚不需言语，只需一个回牟，便已沁入人心。 自然的美，通常都不会太在意芬芳所及，而是引人自寻芳迹。

Women beauty comes with the delicacy and indifference to fame and wealth

Women beauty, with flowers for her appearance, birds for her voice, the Moon for her demeanor, willow for her state, jade for her bone, ice and snow for her muscle, water for her attitude, and the poetry for her heart. Women beauty, just as natural landscape, calm and peaceful, indifference to fame and wealth; a beautiful women like a forest in the morning, light rain just breaks, slim twined with delicate; although not stunning, aura comes from inside, just like the Phantom in crystals; the charm needs no words, but a look back, remains in hearts forever. Natural beauty usually does not care whether it is known to people, but people search for it from all over the world.
